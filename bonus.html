Kingston High School

Kingston was the first mascot of the NHL's Los Angeles Kings. He was a snow leopard who was around for the 1994 season. After being replaced by Bailey, he was brought out of retirement to become the mascot for the Kings AHL affiliate, the Ontario Reign.
